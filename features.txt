APP'S OPERATIONS

--------------------------------------------------------------------
User's related operations
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Register - /register
Login - /login
Logout - /logout
Update profile - /user/profile/update
Change password - /user/password/change
Delete account - /user/account/delete
Delete account (Admin) - /admin/user/:id/account/delete
--------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------
Ad's related operations
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Add advertisement - /ad/new
Read all Ads - /ads/all/:pageNum
Read one Ad - /ad/:id
Read image of an Ad - /ad/:id/image/:index
Read preview image of an Ad - /ad/:id/preview/image
Edit an Ad - /ad/:id/edit
Delete an Ad - /ad/:id/remove
Delete anyone's Ad (Admin) - /admin/ad/:id/remove
Search an Ad - /ads/search/:pageNo
--------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------
Report's related operations
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Report an Ad - /report/:id/post
Read all the reports (Admin) - /reports/all/:pageNum
--------------------------------------------------------------------
